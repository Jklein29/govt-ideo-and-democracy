))) OVERVIEW OF "CODEBOOK PART 4: PRIMARY ELECTORAL DISTRICTS"
===========================================================================

Part 4 of the CSES Codebook presents the primary electoral districts by
polity, which refer to the constituency/riding/district in which an 
individual respondent in a survey votes. 
These value labels refer to coders reported in variable 
D2032 "PRIMARY ELECTORAL DISTRICT" detailed in CSES Codebook Part 2. 

These districts are sometimes identified "indirectly," through postal 
codes, etc... by the CSES Secretariat with the assistance of the 
national collaborators. Where postal codes, etc., were ambiguous,
cases are coded missing. Wherever possible, CSES uses the labels used 
by each country's electoral commission. 

Only districts where respondents are in the CSES data are listed (see 
second column of Table below). Polities are listed in alphabetical order.
